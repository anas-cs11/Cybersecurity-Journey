# Introduction to Cybersecurity
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting digital assets such as data, systems, and networks from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. The main goal is to ensure that information remains secure, reliable, and accessible only to authorized users.
## Key Concepts
- Asset protection (data, systems, networks)
- Prevent unauthorized access
- Maintain system security and reliability  
## CIA Triad
- Confidentiality → restrict access to authorized users
- Integrity → ensure data is accurate and unchanged
- Availability → ensure systems/data are accessible  
## Benefits of Cybersecurity
- Protects sensitive information
- Prevents financial and data loss
- Builds trust and system reliability  
## Blue Team Roles
- Defend systems and networks
- Monitor activities and detect threats
- Respond to security incidents  
## Security Analyst Responsibilities
- Monitor logs and alerts
- Identify and analyze threats
- Respond to incidents
- Improve security measures
