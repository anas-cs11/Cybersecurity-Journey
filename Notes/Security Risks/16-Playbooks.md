## **1. What is a Security Playbook?**

A **Playbook** is a manual that provides a consistent list of actions to take during a security event.

- **The Goal:** To ensure **Urgency, Efficiency, and Accuracy** regardless of which analyst is on duty.
- **Types:** Incident Response (IR), Security Alerts, Team-specific, and Product-specific.

---

## **2. The Incident Response (IR) Playbook**

**Incident Response (IR)** is an organization's quick attempt to identify, contain, and fix a security breach. It follows a **6-phase process**:

## **Phase 1: Preparation**

- **Action:** Building the foundation *before* an attack.
- **Details:** Documenting procedures, hiring the right staff, and educating users.
- **Analyst Role:** Knowing your specific "Role and Responsibility" in the plan.

## **Phase 2: Detection and Analysis**

- **Action:** Using technology to see if a breach actually happened.
- **Details:** Analyzing the "magnitude" (how big is the problem?) using **SIEM (Security Information and Event Management)** tools.

## **Phase 3: Containment (High Priority)**

- **Action:** Stopping the "bleeding."
- **Details:** Taking immediate steps to prevent the threat from spreading to other data or systems.

## **Phase 4: Eradication and Recovery**

- **Action:** Cleaning up and getting back to work.
- **Details:** Removing malicious code (Eradication) and restoring systems to a secure state (**IT (Information Technology)** Restoration).

## **Phase 5: Post-Incident Activity**

- **Action:** The "Lessons Learned" phase.
- **Details:** Documenting the root cause and informing leadership so the organization is better prepared next time.

## **Phase 6: Coordination**

- **Action:** Sharing information and reporting.
- **Details:** Ensuring the organization meets **Compliance** requirements (legal rules) and keeps all necessary teams in the loop.

---

## **3. How SIEM and Playbooks Work Together**

Think of the **SIEM (Security Information and Event Management)** as the **Smoke Detector** and the **Playbook** as the **Fire Drill**:

1. The **SIEM** detects a threat and sends an **Alert**.
2. The **Security Analyst** receives the alert.
3. The Analyst opens the correct **Playbook** to guide them through the 6 phases of response
